Months 1-3 | $0-500 investment

  • Printful (recommended)
  • Printify (backup option)
  • Zero inventory risk
  • Test colors/sizes with real data

Phase 2: Small Batch

Months 3-6 | $2-10K investment

  • AS Colour blanks + local embroidery
  • Bella+Canvas Direct
  • 100-300 unit runs
  • Better margins on proven sellers

Tee cost: ~$12-16 | Sell: $34 | Profit: $15-20

Phase 3: Bulk Manufacturing

Months 7-12 | $15-30K investment

  • Royal Apparel (US, ethical)
  • LA Apparel (premium)
  • Bangladesh/Vietnam (cost)
  • 500-1000+ unit runs

Tee cost: ~$9 | Sell: $34 | Profit: $20

Pre-Launch (8-12 Weeks)
Week 1-2

Brand Foundation

Finalize logo, visual identity. Secure domain. Get vector logo files (.ai/.svg).

Week 3-4

Product Development

Design first collection (12-15 pieces). Order 3-5 samples. Source manufacturers.

Week 5-6

Content & Platform

Build Shopify store. Create 50+ content pieces. Set up Instagram/TikTok.

Week 7-8

Community Building

Recruit 10 brand ambassadors. Set up email marketing (Klaviyo). Build waitlist.

Week 9-10

Soft Launch

Friends & family test run. Work out bugs. Gather feedback.

Launch Week + First 90 Days
Launch Day

Go Live

Email blast to waitlist. All influencer posts go live. Meta/TikTok ads launch. 20% off first 48 hours.

Week 1-4

Momentum

Daily social content (3-5 posts/day). Weekly email to list. PR push to coastal/surf media.

Week 5-8

Growth Tactics

Retargeting campaigns. Email flows (welcome, abandoned cart). Heavy UGC push.

Week 9-12

Scale & Learn

Monitor metrics (CAC, ROAS, AOV). Plan next collection drop. Transition proven sellers to small batch.
